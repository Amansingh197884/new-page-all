Ace Estate Plots

Ace Estate Plots is a thoughtfully planned plotted development located in Sector 22D, Yamuna Expressway, offering premium plots for sale near Jewar Airport. Spread across 80 acres, this development is perfect for investors looking for plots in Yamuna Expressway Authority with high potential returns.

Set amidst future-ready infrastructure and green zones, this project is ideal for constructing low-rise villas or second homes in a tranquil setting close to the city. With the proximity to Noida International Airport and Eastern Peripheral Expressway, this area is a key part of Yamuna Expressway’s real estate boom.
